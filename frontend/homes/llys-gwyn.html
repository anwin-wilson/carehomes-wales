About Llys Gwyn

A True Home, Not Just Care

Llys Gwyn Residential Home is a warm, welcoming environment where residents are treated as individuals — with their own histories, preferences, and personalities celebrated every day. We offer a true home-from-home experience, balancing attentive personal care with genuine independence.

Our dedicated staff team maintains strong continuity of care, building lasting bonds with residents and their families. Quality assurance is central to our ethos — we are fully CIW registered and regularly assessed.

We aim to make every resident's day enriching and joyful. From lively social activities to quiet moments of personal reflection, Llys Gwyn caters for all temperaments and care needs.
