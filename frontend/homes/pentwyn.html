About Pentwyn House

Dual-Registered Care at Its Finest

Pentwyn House Nursing Home will be a CIW-registered dual facility offering both nursing and residential care — the only home you'll ever need to consider for your loved one.

Why Pentwyn House?

Pentwyn House will carry the same Valley Care Group values that have made Glan-yr-Afon and Llys Gwyn so beloved — but with modern facilities and an expanded range of specialist services, purpose-built for 21st century care provision.

Located in Cardiff, we will serve the local community with unmatched compassion, professionalism, and love.
