About Glan-yr-Afon

Specialist Nursing Care in Blackwood

Glan-yr-Afon Nursing Home is set in beautiful Blackwood countryside and provides a warm, caring environment for residents who require nursing, residential, or specialist care. Our highly qualified team delivers round-the-clock support with an unwavering commitment to dignity, comfort, and person-centred care.

Registered and regularly inspected by the Care Inspectorate Wales (CIW), we are proud of our outstanding reputation — with a remarkable 9.9 rating and over 41 reviews on carehome.co.uk.

We work closely with local NHS services including the Wound Research Team at UHW, the Palliative Care Team at St Davids, and the Marie Curie Centre, Holme Towers to ensure expert, co-ordinated care for every resident.

Life at Glan-yr-Afon

🌅

A Day in the Home

Residents enjoy a fulfilling, structured daily routine while retaining the freedom to make personal choices. Each day is enriched with activities, social time, and individual care moments.

🍽️

Meals & Nutrition

Our catering team prepares freshly cooked meals daily, catering for all dietary needs and personal preferences. Great food is central to quality of life.

🎭

Activities & Fun

Our Activities Organiser coordinates a weekly programme including music & movement, keep fit, games, quizzes, bingo, outings, and seasonal parties.

Families are warmly welcomed at any time. A quiet private lounge is available for personal visits. We encourage residents to maintain all their cherished relationships.
